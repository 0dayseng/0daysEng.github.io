Training course: "Parallels Desktop Internals"

2-day specialized training focused on undocumented and low-level system internals of Parallels Desktop hypervisor for Mac. Key subsystems of Parallels Desktop software as they map to the universal Hypervisor Threat Model will be discussed, as well as reverse engineering techniques specific to this system.

The content of this training heavily relies on the abstract models and knowledge introduced in the Hypervisor Vulnerability Research training. Prior knowledge of both low-level virtualization technologies and general reverse engineering practices are recommended as a prerequisite.

Intermediate to Advanced
